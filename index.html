Koi fish are what you might call a happy accident. In the 1600s, Japanese carp farmers began noticing unusual color variations among the fish. "The popular koi fish of today are actually ornamental varietals of domesticated carp," Hilstrom says".While koi were originally bred for consumption, like all other carp, farmers began breeding them exclusively as pets in the 1800s, thanks to their uniquely stunning appearance

Yellow Devil Fish

Assessor flavissimus, the yellow devilfish or yellow scissortail, is a fish from the western Central Pacific, recorded from the Great Barrier Reef and Papua New Guinea. It occasionally makes its way into the aquarium trade. It grows to a size of 5.5 cm in length.

Gold Fish

Goldfish are golden yellow or orange fish that live as pets in fish tanks and man-made ponds. They are freshwater fish so they can't live in salty water. They need large enough tanks or ponds to live in and be healthy, and enjoy having other goldfish around them. They are omnivores, eating both plants and animals

Goldfish originally came from China over 1,000 years ago. There are now hundreds of different types of goldfish around the world that come in many different combinations and shades of yellow and orange
